PRIVACY POLICY - Arkalia CIA
Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

Arkalia CIA ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. DATA COLLECTION

Arkalia CIA is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- NO DATA COLLECTED: We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.
- LOCAL STORAGE ONLY: All your data (documents, reminders, contacts) is stored exclusively on your device.
- NO ANALYTICS: We do not use analytics tools or tracking services.
- NO CLOUD SYNC: Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

3. DATA STORAGE

All data is stored locally on your device using:
- Encrypted SQLite database (AES-256 encryption)
- Secure file storage with device-level encryption
- No cloud backup or synchronization

4. PERMISSIONS

Arkalia CIA requests the following permissions:

- Calendar Access: To create and manage reminders
- Contacts Access: To manage emergency contacts (ICE)
- Storage Access: To save and manage documents
- Notifications: To deliver reminder notifications

All permissions are optional and the app can function with limited features if permissions are denied.

5. DATA SECURITY

- AES-256 encryption for all stored data
- Device-level security (Keychain/Keystore)
- No network transmission of personal data
- Local-only data processing

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Arkalia CIA does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

Since all data is stored locally:
- You have full control over your data
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- You can export your data at any time
- No account required, no data sharing

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on our GitHub repository.
